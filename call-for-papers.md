## Submission

A call for research papers will be issued, focusing on innovative methods in AI-driven cultural heritage preservation. All submissions will adhere to the ICCV 2025 paper submission style, format, and length restrictions. The review process will follow a strict timeline to ensure that all accepted papers meet the camera-ready deadline for inclusion in the official proceedings. ## Topics
- AI and computer vision for cultural heritage
- Multimodal learning with 3D scans, documents, and video
- Image and 3D generation for cultural artifacts
- Ethical and social aspects of digitizing artistic legacies
